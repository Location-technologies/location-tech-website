# 2. Introduction
Welcome to **SportsMania**, developed by Location Tech. SportsMania is a dual-role platform connecting athletes (Players) with sports venue owners (Venue Owners) to discover, book, and manage sports facilities.

We are deeply committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your data is handled securely. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our services. By downloading, accessing, or using the SportsMania app, you signify that you have read, understood, and agree to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy.

---

# 3. Definitions

*   **Personal Data:** Any information that relates to an identified or identifiable living individual (e.g., name, phone number, email).
*   **Usage Data:** Data collected automatically, either generated by the use of the Service or from the Service infrastructure itself (e.g., the duration of a page visit).
*   **Location Data:** Information regarding the physical location of your device or the venues listed on our platform.
*   **Service:** The SportsMania mobile application and related backend infrastructure.
*   **User:** The individual accessing or using the Service, acting as either a Player or a Venue Owner.
*   **Account:** A unique account created for you to access our Service.

---

# 4. Information You Collect

**Personal Information:**
*   Phone Number (used as your primary identifier)
*   Full Name (optional/profile info)
*   Email Address (optional)
*   Password (securely hashed, never stored in plain text)
*   Profile Picture
*   OTP Verification codes

**Device Information:**

*   Operating System (iOS or Android)

**Location Information:**
*   City/Location of venues (provided by Venue Owners)
*   Approximate or precise device location (to show nearby sports venues)

**Payment Information:**
*   Transaction IDs, booking costs, and refund statuses
*   Subscription status (for Venue Owners)
*   *Note: We do not store complete credit card or banking details. These are handled securely by our integrated payment providers.*

**Uploaded Content:**
*   Images (Venue Owners uploading high-resolution photos of their facilities)

**Local Storage:**
*   Secure Tokens (JWT Authentication tokens stored securely via `expo-secure-store`)

# 5. Why You Collect Information

We collect your information for the following purposes:
*   **Account Creation & Authentication:** To register users, send OTPs via SMS, and secure your session.
*   **Booking Management:** To facilitate pitch reservations, manage operational hours, and process scheduling.
*   **Payments & Payouts:** To securely process booking fees, owner commissions, and potential refunds.
*   **Customer Support:** To assist you with account issues, booking conflicts, or technical problems.
*   **Notifications:** To send important booking reminders, manual review alerts, and system updates.
*   **Analytics & Service Improvement:** To understand how users interact with our UI, optimize our dynamic pricing rules, and improve app stability.
*   **Security & Fraud Prevention:** To protect against unauthorized access and ensure platform integrity.

# 6. Legal Basis

For users in jurisdictions where legal basis definitions apply, we process your data based on:
*   **Contract Fulfillment:** Processing is necessary to provide the Service you requested (e.g., booking a pitch).

# 7. Permissions Used

To provide our core features, the SportsMania app may request the following device permissions:
*   **Camera & Photos (Storage):** Required for Venue Owners to select and upload images of their venues to the gallery.
*   **Notifications:** Required to send push notifications for booking reminders and status updates.
*   **Location:** Used to dynamically fetch and display the nearest sports venues to your current area.
*   **Network Access:** Required for the app to communicate with our backend servers.

---

# 8. How Information is Shared

We share your information strictly to operate our platform. We share data with:
*   **Payment Providers:** To process transactions securely.
*   **SMS Providers:** To send OTP verification codes.
*   **Cloud Hosting & Storage:** To host our databases and store uploaded venue images securely.
*   **Other Users:** If you are a Player, the Venue Owner will see your booking details. If you are a Venue Owner, your venue details and location are visible to all Players.

*We do not sell your personal information to third parties.*

# 9. Third-Party Services

We utilize the following external services to power SportsMania. Each service is governed by its own privacy policy:
*   **Expo / EAS:** For app framework, notifications, and over-the-air updates.
*   **Twilio:** For delivering SMS/OTP messages.
*   **AWS (Amazon Web Services):** Specifically AWS S3 for secure cloud storage of venue images.
*   **PostgreSQL / Prisma:** For our secure backend database management.
*   **Payment Gateways (e.g., JazzCash, EasyPaisa, or Stripe):** For processing financial transactions securely.
*   **Mapbox / OpenStreetMap:** For rendering maps and calculating spatial geodata for venues.

# 10. Data Retention

*   **Active Accounts:** We retain your Personal Data, booking history, and venue information for as long as your account is active.
*   **Deletion:** Upon account deletion, your personal data is purged from our active databases.
*   **Financial Records:** Certain transaction data may be retained for longer periods to comply with legal, tax, or financial reporting obligations.

---

# 11. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data, including:
*   HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data transmitted between the app and our servers.
*   Password hashing using bcrypt.
*   Secure authentication using JSON Web Tokens (JWT) stored in your device's `expo-secure-store` keychain/keystore.
*   Role-based access controls on our backend to protect owner and player data.

*However, please be aware that no electronic transmission or storage system can guarantee 100% absolute security.*
